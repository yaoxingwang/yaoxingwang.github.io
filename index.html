Yaoxing Wang

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Unmanned Systems Technology Research Institute of Northwestern Polytechnical University, supervised by Ass. Prof. Shan Gao. Prior to that, I received my Master of Electronic Information degree in computer technology from the School of Information Engineering at Ningxia University in 2024, supervised by Prof. Hao Liu. In 2022, I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree in computer science and technology at Ningxia University, Yinchuan, China.

My research interests mainly include computer vision, network management, and mathematical optimization.
